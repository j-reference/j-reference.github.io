Say goodbye to manual citation

j-Reference allows users to paste research article links from ScienceDirect (since all other TJ-library supported databases provide citations) and automatically create an APA or MLA citation where one can paste into noodletools or other texteditors


Future features will include a webapp version, saving and organizing multiple citations, and support for other sites.




Important:

To make sure your downloaded copy works, copy this URL and put it into the text bar.
All articles you want to cite must be in the exact format of this article.

https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0378112717313002
